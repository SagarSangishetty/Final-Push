The Problem

Service exposed port: 8080 (what you exposed to the outside)
Your readiness probe: Checking http://pod-ip:8080/ ❌

The probe was trying to reach port 8080 inside the container, but the React app was only listening on port 3000, so you got "connection refused."
The Fix
You corrected the readiness probe to use port 3000 (the actual port the app listens on inside the container):

