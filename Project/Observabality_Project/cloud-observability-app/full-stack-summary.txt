namespace: monitoring
├── Prometheus + Grafana
└── Ingress at /grafana (order: 1)

namespace: logging
├── Elasticsearch (shared)
├── Fluentbit (collects logs)
├── Kibana (views logs)
└── Ingress at /kibana (order: 2)

namespace: tracing
├── OTel Collector
├── Jaeger (uses Elasticsearch for storage)
└── Ingress at / (order: 0 or no order, Jaeger at root)

namespace: app
├── Your FastAPI app
└── Ingress at / (order: 10)

One ALB
├── /grafana → Grafana
├── /kibana → Kibana
├── / (priority 0) → Jaeger
└── / (priority 10) → App
