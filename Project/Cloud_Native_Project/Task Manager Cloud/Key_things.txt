Key Things to Replace Before Deploying
In k8s/base/backend-deployment.yaml and ingress.yaml, search for these placeholders:

<YOUR_ECR_ACCOUNT_ID> and <REGION> → your ECR image URIs
<ACCOUNT_ID> and role ARN → from your eksctl create iamserviceaccount output
<CERT_ID> → from ACM after you request/validate a certificate
tasks.yourdomain.com → your Route53 domain
